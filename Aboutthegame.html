OUT IN FEBURARY!!


This cutting edge game begins with you mowing your customers lawn. Unfortunately, it slowly dawns on you that you have left your keys indoors and you're locked out of the house. At that moment, a mob of zombies, who don't have hayfever (not that they would care anyway!) swarm into your back garden and you are under attack. How could this even happen on a Saturday?! All you have to defend yourself is your trusty and very rusty lawnmower. As the waves of zombie attacks keep coming you have to race to your mysterious client's (Gerald's) mansion to upgrade your mower. Who knows what the zombies want from you... find out if you dare!
